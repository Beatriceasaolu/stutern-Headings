Padding, Margin and Border emerge from the concept of the Box Model Layout.

The following is the difference between them.

  • Padding: This is the space between the content and the border. Giving a padding to a semantics creates a space between the border and the content of that semantics
  • Border: This is the space between the inner content and the outer content. Basically, Border lies between margin and padding or the actual content.
  • Margin: This is the outer space of the html semantics. Giving a margin to a html semantics creates a space between the border or the actual content and the rest of the element in the body of the html page.
